FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is TrueKYC?

TrueKYC is a comprehensive web-based SaaS solution designed to simplify the KYC (Know Your Customer) and KYB (Know Your Business) verification and onboarding process for businesses of all sizes. Our platform offers automated verification, customizable verification levels, remote verification through QR links, transaction monitoring, and detailed statistics and reporting to ensure compliance and enhance customer onboarding experiences.

What makes TrueKYC different from other KYC verification services?

TrueKYC sets itself apart through its comprehensive suite of features tailored to modern compliance needs. Unlike other services, TrueKYC offers customizable verification levels, long-distance verification capabilities, detailed transaction monitoring, and a versatile user management system accommodating multiple user types with different permissions. Our focus on flexibility, security, and ease of use ensures that businesses can meet regulatory requirements efficiently while providing a seamless onboarding experience for their customers.

Can TrueKYC handle verifications for international customers?

Yes, TrueKYC is equipped to handle KYC and KYB verifications for customers across the globe. Our remote verification feature, enabled through secure QR links, allows businesses to perform long-distance verifications effortlessly, thus expanding their customer base without compromising security or compliance standards.

How can I get started with TrueKYC??

Getting started with TrueKYC is simple. Visit our website and choose the subscription plan that best fits your business needs. Our plans are designed to accommodate businesses of all sizes, from individual professionals to large organizations. Once you sign up, you'll have access to all the features of TrueKYC.
